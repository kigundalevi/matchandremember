Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 5, 2025

Simple Summary: Match & Remember is a completely offline children's game. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. No data ever leaves your device.

1. Introduction

Match & Remember ("we", "our", or "the app") is a card matching game designed for children ages 3-6. This privacy policy explains our commitment to protecting your privacy.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

Match & Remember does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Photos, camera, or microphone access
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Any other personal or sensitive information

3. How the App Works

Match & Remember is a fully offline application that:

  • Stores game preferences locally on your device only (category selection, difficulty level, sound settings)
  • Does not require an internet connection to function
  • Does not communicate with any external servers
  • Does not contain any third-party tracking or analytics

4. Local Storage

The app uses local device storage (AsyncStorage) to remember:

  • Your last selected game category (Animals, Shapes, or Numbers)
  • Your last selected difficulty level (Easy, Medium, or Hard)
  • Sound on/off preference

This data never leaves your device and is only used to improve your gameplay experience.

5. Third-Party Services

Match & Remember does not integrate with any third-party services, including:

  • No advertising networks
  • No analytics platforms
  • No social media integrations
  • No payment processors

6. Children's Privacy

Match & Remember is designed specifically for children and complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not knowingly collect any information from children or anyone else. The app:

  • Contains no advertisements
  • Contains no in-app purchases
  • Contains no external links
  • Does not require parental consent (as no data is collected)

7. Permissions

Match & Remember does not request any device permissions. The app does not access:

  • Camera or photos
  • Microphone
  • Location services
  • Contacts
  • Internet/network access (beyond initial download)

8. Data Security

Since we do not collect any data, there is no data to secure or protect from unauthorized access. All game preferences are stored locally on your device and are protected by your device's security measures.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app and on this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal information, there is no data to access, modify, or delete. You can uninstall the app at any time to remove all locally stored preferences.
